About

Vila Calu is a small neighborhood located on the outskirts of the southern zone of the city of São Paulo-SP, very close to the border with the municipality of Itapecerica da Serra. It is part of the M'Boi Mirim subprefecture.

Like other neighborhoods on the outskirts of São Paulo, it has a busy local commercial center, schools, a health center, and public transportation services. The main access to the neighborhood is via Estrada do M'Boi Mirim.

The region was largely populated by immigrants from the northeast of Brazil and the hinterlands of Minas Gerais, who came to São Paulo to work in the industry in the late 1970s and early 1980s, as a low-cost housing option due to the distance from the city center.
